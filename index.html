Our vision is to promote sustainable scalable solutions and a balanced approach to people, planet and profit.

CIRCULAR ECONOMY

We aim to create 5000 jobs for women in low-income areas, providing a means of livelihood beyond minimum wage.

We are deploying river cleanup solutions to 25 rivers next year and launching an AI boat to clean the Great Pacific Garbage Patch.

Our unique business model generates revenue while contributing to our circular economy ecosystem mission.

Documentary and animated TV Series

Join us on a thrilling ocean voyage, "The 1st 365 Days," captured on film for Netflix, Amazon Prime, and other platforms.

We're also excited to launch an animated series that will inspire kids to protect our oceans and marine life. Come be a part of the movement to preserve our planet's most vital natural resource!

Interactive gaming

Come aboard and help us raise awareness for real-world ocean problems through interactive gaming. Our gamification efforts will be available on mobile, console, and PC platforms for both kids and adults. Let's work together to solve these challenges!

Education for a better tomorrow

Join the Oceans & Us Global Academy, a digital platform that connects students and professors to collaborate on saving our oceans. Access research on marine life, plastic pollution, recycling innovations, and climate change solutions.

People + Planet

As a ‘Network Orchestrator’, we will continue to innovate at a fast pace without reinventing the wheel, by crowdsourcing existing solutions through Partnerships.

We will give people the opportunity to participate towards making a difference, while owning a piece of ‘Oceans & Us’, through an ‘Oceans Initiative’ Crowdfunding campaign.

Jobs for good

In collaboration with our local Partners, we aim to create over 5000 job opportunities for women in low-income geographies.

We will also conduct local Community drives, with an aim to provide a means to livelihood, ensuring beyond minimum wages.

Plastic innovation labs

We will study and test different material gathered from the Oceans and Rivers to understand the effect and impact of time, weather and salts on product recyclability and reusability.

Partnerships with Corporates will help them develop new product lines with recycled material.

Data for good

The Data we gather on the different materials in the Ocean will help us categorize the products contributing to Ocean Pollution the most.

This in turn, will enable us to directly work with the respective manufacturing brands and Policy Makers towards Waste Management and Recycling initiatives.

Revenue from Good

The "OCEANS & US" initiative creates revenue through waste management partnerships, waste-to-energy conversion, plastic credit trading, NFTs, IP licensing, sponsorship, sustainable merchandise, and ESG partnerships, all while contributing to river and ocean cleanups and creating a circular economy ecosystem.

Our unique business model aligns profit with a commitment to sustainability.

The OCEANS & US initiative has collaborated with renowed NFT artists to produce their own NFT series

FAQ

Discover how OCEANS & US aims to tackle plastic pollution and promote environmental sustainability.

Which are the phases of the project?

Phase one (Riverbanks)

Our clean up solutions will be deployed on the riverbanks of India,
collecting waste through the conveyor belt of the boat.

Phase two (Ocean cleanup)

Our end Goal is to deploy multiple Autonomous AI Cleaning Boats
in and around the Great Pacific Garbage Patch.

How do we produce revenue?

We create revenue through waste management partnerships, waste-to-energy conversion, plastic credit trading, NFTs, IP licensing, sponsorship, sustainable merchandise, and ESG partnerships. Our business model aligns profit with a commitment to sustainability while contributing to river and ocean cleanups and creating a circular economy ecosystem.

What is the target market for "Oceans & Us" initiative?

"Oceans & Us" initiative has a global target market, with a focus on India, Egypt, and UAE. We aim to bring together individuals, communities, and organizations from around the world who share our vision of protecting the planet and creating a sustainable future.

How can I contribute to the project?

You can contribute to our mission towards a more sustainable future by donating one hour or one dollar per month. To do so, simply fill out the contact form on our website and we'll be in touch with further details on how you can get involved. Together, we can make a difference in preserving our planet for future generations through recycling and regenerative finance.
